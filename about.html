About JDRC17

About the Conference / 研究会について

The Japan Drosophila Research Conference (JDRC) is an annual meeting dedicated to researchers studying Drosophila melanogaster and related species. Since its inaugural meeting, JDRC has provided a vibrant forum for scientists at all career stages—from graduate students to senior investigators—to present their latest findings, discuss current challenges, and build collaborative networks.

日本ショウジョウバエ研究会（JDRC）は、ショウジョウバエを研究モデルとする国内外の研究者が集う年次集会です。学生から教員まで幅広い参加者が最新の研究成果を発表・議論し、新たな共同研究の創出を目指しています。

JDRC17 × Japan-Taiwan Fly Symposium 2026

For the first time, JDRC17 will be jointly held with the Japan-Taiwan Fly Symposium 2026, bringing together Drosophila researchers from Japan and Taiwan for three days of talks, posters, and networking events. We look forward to strengthening the ties between the two research communities.

今回は初めて日台ショウジョウバエシンポジウム2026との共同開催となります。日本・台湾双方の研究者が一堂に会し、活発な科学的交流が行われることを期待しています。
